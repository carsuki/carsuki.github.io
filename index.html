About This Website

Questions

What is this?

my personal site. it's supposed to look like a somewhat faithful recreation of a Macintosh Plus running System 7 (with a couple liberties taken)

Why an old Mac?

I like Macs and I use a Mac. and I think there are more than enough websites out there that look like Windows 98!

So what can I do here?

honestly not much right now. aesthetics are a lot easier than thinking of actual content for this place...

you can drag the windows around, look at the different menu options, and check out the GIF library under Extras. in there you can see every GIF in use on the site. (you get a random background each time this page loads)
